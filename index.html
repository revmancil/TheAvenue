Our Church Family

Welcome to
THE AVENUE

For over 64 years, Avenue Progressive Baptist Church has been a beacon of faith, hope, and love in South Dallas. We are a Word-centered, praise-centered, soul-winning community committed to building disciples and serving our neighbors with the love of Christ.

Whether you're searching for spiritual growth, a community that truly cares, or a place to make a real difference — you'll find a genuine welcome here. Come as you are.

First Time Here?

What to Expect
on Sunday

We want your first visit to be easy, comfortable, and memorable. Here's what a typical Sunday looks like at THE AVENUE.

01

Friendly Welcome

Our hospitality team greets you at the door and helps you feel at home from the moment you arrive. You are expected and valued here.

02

Uplifting Worship

Spirit-led music that prepares your heart for the Word. Come ready to worship God with your whole being — voice, heart, and soul.

03

Biblical Preaching

Pastor Carroll delivers expository, practical messages from Scripture that speak directly to your everyday life and spiritual journey.

04

Real Community

Stay after service to connect with people who genuinely care. There's always a warm conversation and a fresh pot of coffee waiting.
