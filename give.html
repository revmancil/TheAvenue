The Heart Behind Giving

Why We Give

At THE AVENUE, we believe giving is an act of worship — a tangible expression of our trust in God and our commitment to His mission. The Bible teaches that everything we have belongs to God, and that we are stewards, not owners, of His resources.

Your tithes and offerings make possible everything that happens at THE AVENUE — from Sunday worship to community outreach, from youth ministry to pastoral care. Every dollar given is invested in eternity.

Common Questions

Giving FAQ

Have a question about giving, tax receipts, or financial accountability? We're committed to full transparency and excellent stewardship of every dollar entrusted to THE AVENUE.

Questions?

Contact our church office for any questions about your giving, statements, or our financial policies.

Contact the Office
Is my gift tax-deductible?

Yes. Avenue Progressive Baptist Church is a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization. All contributions made to the church are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. You will receive a written acknowledgment for all gifts over $250.

How do I get a year-end giving statement?

Annual giving statements are mailed or emailed each January for the prior year. Contact the church office at info@avenuepbc.org or 469-372-0065 if you need a statement at any time.

Is online giving secure?

Yes, completely. Our online giving platform uses industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your personal and financial information. We never store your payment data on our servers.

How is my giving used?

Every dollar given to THE AVENUE is used to support ministry operations, pastor and staff compensation, facility maintenance, ministry programs, community outreach, and mission support. We are committed to faithful stewardship and financial transparency.

Can I give to a specific ministry or fund?

Yes. You can designate your gift for the Building Fund, Benevolence Fund, Youth Ministry, or other specific areas. Contact the church office or note the designation on your check or online gift.
