Elected May 2025

Dr. Mancil Carroll III was elected Senior Pastor of Avenue Progressive Baptist Church in May 2025. He brings to THE AVENUE a deep passion for expository preaching, disciple-making, and community transformation rooted in a sincere love for Jesus Christ and His people.

With a commitment to biblical truth and practical ministry, Pastor Carroll's preaching is known for being both scripturally grounded and powerfully relevant to the everyday lives of his congregation. He leads with humility, vision, and a servant's heart.

Pastoral Vision

Leading THE AVENUE
Into the Future

Dr. Carroll's vision for THE AVENUE is anchored in four convictions: faithful proclamation of the Word, authentic community, compassionate service, and multiplication of disciples across generations.

"My prayer is that THE AVENUE will be a church where the lost find salvation, the broken find healing, and the faithful find purpose."

— Dr. Mancil Carroll III
01

Faithful Preaching

Every message is grounded in the authoritative Word of God, delivered with clarity, conviction, and pastoral care for every listener.

02

Discipleship First

The goal is not just attendance but transformation — believers growing into mature disciples who love God and serve others.

03

Community Service

THE AVENUE exists not only for those within its walls but for the entire South Dallas community. Practical ministry matters.

04

Next Generation Focus

Investing in the youth, young adults, and families of tomorrow — equipping the next generation to carry the faith forward.
